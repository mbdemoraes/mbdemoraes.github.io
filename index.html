Career Summary

Matheus B. de Moraes is a postdoctoral researcher at the Faculdade de Tecnologia (FT) of the Universidade Estadual de Campinas (Unicamp), and a member of the Energy Production Innovation Center (EPIC) and the High-Performance Intelligent Decision Systems (HighPIDS) research group.


He received his PhD degree in technology, with an emphasis on information and communication systems, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Guilherme Palermo Coelho at FT/Unicamp in 2023. In his PhD thesis, he worked on bio-inspired optimization, machine learning, and decision analysis, which he applied to petroleum well placement optimization.

During his PhD studies, Matheus participated in an exchange program at the University of Stavanger (UiS) in Norway during the first semester of 2023. During this period, he was supervised by Prof. Dr. Reidar Brumer Bratvold at the Department of Energy Resources, where he participated in Prof. Reidar's research group, Decisions & Data Analytics, to develop a decision-making methodology for the petroleum industry.

Matheus is an active member of the international community, being a member of the IEEE and IEEE Computational Intelligence Society , as well as a reviewer for several international journals such as The Journal of Supercomputing, IEEE Latin America Transactions, Expert Systems with Applications, and Information Sciences.

Matheus has a degree in systems analysis and development and a master's degree in technology, which he received in 2015 and 2019, respectively.
